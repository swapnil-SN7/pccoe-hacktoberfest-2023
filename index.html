Timeline for the Event

Date : 18 oct 2023

11:00 AM to 11:15 AM
Welcome Address

✨ Welcome to Hacktoberfest 2023! We're thrilled to have you here 🚀
Get ready for a day of learning, collaboration, and coding fun 🧑🏻‍🏫

11:15 AM to 11:30 AM
Introduction to Hacktoberfest & Opensource

👉 Learn about Hacktoberfest and why open source matters.
👉 Discover how your contributions can make a difference.
👉 Stay engaged, ask questions, and get ready to dive into the world of open source.

11:30 AM to 12:00PM
Workshop for Opensource Contribution

👉 This workshop will guide you on how to make your first open source contribution.
👉 Follow the step-by-step instructions provided and don't be afraid to ask for help.
👉 By the end, you'll be ready to start contributing to open source projects.

12:00 PM to 12:30 PM
Opportunities to contribute

👉 Explore various open source projects and find one that interests you. 👉 Take the time to explore a diverse range of open source projects, including those initiated by ACM-PCCOE.
👉 These projects cater to both beginners and advanced learners, offering opportunities to contribute in various tech stacks such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Data Structures and Algorithms (DSA), animated buttons, and more.
👉 Find a project that aligns with your skills and interests, and start making a meaningful contribution to the open source community.
👉 Feel free to seek guidance, ask questions to our team and get valuable insights into the world of open source.

12:30 PM to 01:30 PM
Breakout Rooms for Icebreaking

👉 This is your chance to meet fellow participants.
👉 Engage in discussions, share your interests, and make new connections.
👉 Building relationships within the tech community is an essential part of your journey.

01:30 PM to 03:30 PM
Get Hacking

👉 Now it's time to put your skills to use.
👉 Work on your selected open source project, and remember to follow best practices.
👉 Don't forget to ask for help or share your progress in the community.

03:30 PM to 04:00 PM
Show and Tell

👉 Share your achievements with the community.
👉 Demonstrate what you've worked on and what you've learned.
👉 It's time to celebrate your contributions and inspire others.

Hacktoberfest is open to everyone in our global community.
Hacktoberfest is a month-long celebration of open source software hosted by DigitalOcean Hacktoberfest - 2023 brings together open-source enthusiasts, beginners, and the global developer community and encourages them to contribute to open-source projects. Our mission is to inspire more people to get involved in open source and work together to improve the software we rely on, and powers our world today.

Open source is changing the world - one pull request at a time.
